Aromatic Coffee Plantation

The one of the most visited spot in Madikeri is the coffee Plantation. Madikeri, the city itself is covered with green bedding of coffee plantations and one of the largest manufactures in India. The lush green estate looks just amazing and experience residing in these lush green estates, where in fact the air appears to bring the coffee aroma even.

Amazing View Of Paddy Fields

Paddy cultivation is one of the main crops grown in the southern part of India.Madikeri is one among those places where the people make it as their family occupation. It is widely found on the valley floors with a beautiful greenery view which is very joyful to take a look around it. This paddy field also leads for the cold climatic conditions of Madikeri.

Rafting (Seasonal Adventure Ride)

One of the most popular rafting trip in kodagu. Experiencing the Rafting in barapole river rafting includes ‘raft-worthy’ rapids and trip down the river having the great way to see the countryside. Let your experience cherish for a lifetime. The River Rafting is just 33km journey from Kotekad Plantation Stay.
